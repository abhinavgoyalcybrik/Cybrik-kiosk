Which best describes you?

Domestic student

You are an Australian domestic student if you are:

  • an Australian citizen;
  • an Australian permanent resident;
  • an Australian permanent humanitarian visa holder;
  • a New Zealand citizen.

International student

You are an international student if you are:

  • a temporary resident (visa status) of Australia;
  • a permanent resident (visa status) of New Zealand;
  • a resident or citizen of any other country, intending to study on a student visa.

Streamlined study packages

Fast-track your study abroad or exchange experience with flexible, themed unit packages.

Joining Deakin on exchange or study abroad? Our streamlined packages group undergraduate units by theme to help you build a study plan that suits your interests and schedule.

  • Study abroad and exchange
    • Exchange partners
    • Pre-approved study options
    • Streamlined study packages
    • How to apply for study abroad and exchange

What are streamlined study packages?

Streamlined study packages are groups of undergraduate (bachelor) level units with no prerequisites and no faculty approval required. That means less admin, more freedom.

You can choose from themed study packages like Australiana, AI and Big Data, or Global Business, or build your own plan using our list of pre-approved units.

All units are at undergraduate (bachelor) level and are suitable if you’ve completed Year 12 or equivalent. They're designed to help you settle in quickly and get the most out of your Deakin experience.

How streamlined study packages work

If you're applying as a study abroad or exchange student, you can easily pick from the listed units under each theme – no extra steps required.

Build your study experience with flexible, themed packages

These packages are designed to make planning simple. Just choose the units that suit your interests and schedule.

Deakin offers three intakes each year – Trimester 1, Trimester 2 and Trimester 3 – giving you the flexibility to start your studies when it works best for you. We’ve outlined the upcoming trimester dates to help you plan ahead.

Units are available across Deakin's four campuses in Victoria: Melbourne Burwood, Geelong Waurn Ponds, Geelong Waterfront and Warrnambool. Many units are also offered online, giving you even more options.

How to use the packages

Each theme package below is organised by trimester, so you can easily see what's available when you're planning to study. Simply choose your theme, find your trimester and select your units, checking which campuses suit you.

Campus codes:

  • B = Melbourne Burwood
  • WP = Geelong Waurn Ponds
  • WF = Geelong Waterfront
  • WB = Warrnambool
  • O = Online.
