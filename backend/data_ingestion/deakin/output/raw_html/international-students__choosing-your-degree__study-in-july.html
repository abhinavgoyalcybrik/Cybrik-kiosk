Which best describes you?

Domestic student

You are an Australian domestic student if you are:

  • an Australian citizen;
  • an Australian permanent resident;
  • an Australian permanent humanitarian visa holder;
  • a New Zealand citizen.

International student

You are an international student if you are:

  • a temporary resident (visa status) of Australia;
  • a permanent resident (visa status) of New Zealand;
  • a resident or citizen of any other country, intending to study on a student visa.

Get financial support for your studies in Australia

Tuition fees at Deakin vary depending on the course you choose and the type of student you are. Did you know you might be able to reduce your costs? 

Southeast Asia Priority Bursary

To support the cost of your education in Australia, Deakin University offers a Southeast Asia Priority Bursary of up to AUD$2,000. Students from Indonesia, Vietnam, The Philippines, Cambodia and across the Southeast Asia region are automatically considered.

Priority benefits for your study journey

  • Automatic consideration for all eligible students – receive up to AUD$2,000 toward your degree costs.
  • Get accelerated processing for your application to live at Deakin Residences – our safe, modern on-campus accommodation.
  • Receive priority for your Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) issuance.

Why study at Deakin University?

Deakin University works closely with industry partners to design and deliver degrees that prepare you for success around the world. That’s why our graduates rank us the #1 university in Melbourne for course satisfaction. 

Proven employability

We’re the #1 university in Melbourne for graduate employment. Our degrees are built with industry and endorsed by employers. Combined with industry-experienced academics and real projects, you will graduate with the skills and experience employers want. 

Comprehensive support

Scholarships, study support, career guidance and health and well-being services help keep you focused on success. Because we’re the #1 university in Melbourne for student support, we understand your unique needs and provide support services every step of the way. 

Vibrant student community

Connect with students from Australia and around the world. Participate in diverse events and activities through 150+ clubs and societies. Build lifelong friendships at our welcoming campuses and join the #1 university in Australia for international student happiness. 

Outstanding facilities and resources

No matter where you are along your Deakin journey, you will enjoy practical learning in industry-standard spaces that let you apply your knowledge and perfect your skills. That’s why we’re the #1 university in Melbourne for facilities and resources.
