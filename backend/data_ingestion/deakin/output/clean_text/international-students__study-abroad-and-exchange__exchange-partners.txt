What is an exchange partner?
An exchange partner is a university that has a formal agreement with Deakin, allowing students from that university to study with us in Australia as part of their degree. Likewise, Deakin students can go on exchange to study at these partner universities around the world.
Through a reciprocal exchange agreement with your home university, you can earn academic credit while immersing yourself in Australia’s unique culture and world-class education. It’s an opportunity to broaden your horizons, gain global perspectives and boost your career prospects – all while staying on track with your studies.

Not eligible for exchange? Join us as a study abroad student
If you’re not eligible for exchange, you can still join us through our study abroad program. This option offers flexibility, lets you earn credit towards your degree, experience life at Deakin and immerse yourself in Australia’s vibrant culture, all without needing a formal partnership.
