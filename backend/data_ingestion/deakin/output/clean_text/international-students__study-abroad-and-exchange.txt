Exchange
If your university is an exchange partner with Deakin, you can join us through our exchange program. You will stay enrolled and pay any tuition fees at your home university and earn credit while studying with us in Australia. It’s a great way to immerse yourself in a new culture while staying on track with your degree.
Study abroad
Not from one of our exchange partners? Not a problem. Our study abroad program lets you apply directly to Deakin and pay tuition fees to us. You'll experience the same world-class education and vibrant campus life as local students, while gaining valuable international perspectives.

Frequently asked questions
You might be eligible for our exchange program if your home university has a formal agreement with Deakin. To participate, you’ll need to contact your home university’s international office, as they must nominate you to us. Exchange students cannot apply directly to Deakin. If accepted, you will stay enrolled and pay any tuition fees to your home university.
If you are not nominated for an exchange or your university doesn’t have an exchange agreement with Deakin, you can apply to us as a study abroad student and pay tuition fees to Deakin.
Both options allow you to experience university life in Australia and gain credit towards your degree.
If you apply to Deakin as a study abroad student, your tuition fees are based on your study load. The fees are the same whether you study on campus or online. The main difference is that online students can enrol in as little as 1 or 2 credit points (up to 4), while on-campus students must enrol in either 3 or 4 credit points. Use the table below to check the fees and how many units to select when you apply.
| Study load (credit points) | 2026 fees (AUD) | 2027 fees (AUD) | Units to select when applying |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 credit point study abroad (online only) | $2,900 | $3,000 | Select 2–3 units to ensure preferred options |
| 2 credit points study abroad (online only) | $5,800 | $6,000 | Select 3–4 units to ensure preferred options |
| 3 credit points study abroad (minimum on-campus enrolment) | $8,700 | $9,000 | Select 5–6 units to ensure preferred options |
| 4 credit points study abroad (maximum on-campus enrolment) | $11,600 | $12,000 | Select 6–8 units to ensure preferred options |
Note: When selecting units in your application, have a few backup options in case your top picks can’t be confirmed, and double-check that your units are at the same campus and offered in the right trimester.
Study abroad and exchange students enrolling on campus at Deakin must undertake a full-time study load of minimum 3 credit points and a maximum of 4 credit points each trimester. Most Deakin units are worth 1 credit point each. Virtual exchange and online study abroad students can select between 1 and 4 credit points, depending on their preferred workload.
One Deakin unit generally equates to 3–4 US credits or about 7.5 ECTS in Europe and the UK.
