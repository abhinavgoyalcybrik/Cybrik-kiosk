Where you will study
Deakin University English Language Institute is located at Deakin’s Melbourne Burwood Campus in Victoria and select courses are available online.* When you study an English language course, you have access to Deakin services and facilities, including the library and medical and fitness centres. Plus, enjoy free memberships to the Deakin University English Language Institute Club and Deakin University Student Association.
On-campus learning
The Melbourne Burwood Campus is a welcoming place to connect with a diverse community of students. The campus is accessible to Melbourne's key transport hubs and a 30-minute drive from the city centre.
Online learning
Deakin is a leader in online study, with 40 years of experience in distance and online education. Our English language programs are delivered through our innovative online learning environment. You can access class materials, assessments and interact with your peers and teachers in real-time.

Where to live
Whether you want to live on campus or nearby, there is a room or house to welcome you. Homestay is a great way to practise your English skills while living with a local family. Discover more about your accommodation options and how we can support you.
Study tours
Learning a language doesn't just happen in a classroom. Our study tours are an opportunity for you to improve your English language skills and experience Australia. We offer study tours to universities, private and professional groups and educational and government organisations. You have the option to integrate with our standard programs, or we can tailor the tour to suit your specific needs. For more information about our study tours, please email us.
