Pathways to study at Deakin University in Australia
Our extensive partnerships with international institutions provides the option to start your degree in your home country, then transition to Australia to complete your studies. There are many pathways designed to give you flexibility while keeping you on track for a globally recognised qualification.

Recognition of prior learning
Recognition of prior learning turns your study at our international partner institutions into Deakin University study credit that can reduce your study duration, as well as the cost of your study. Recognition of prior learning pathways provide a smooth and supported transition to study in Australia and enable you to graduate with a globally recognised degree.
Our Recognition of Prior Learning credit calculator can help you determine whether your previous studies in China, India, Sri Lanka, Vietnam or elsewhere are eligible for credit at Deakin.
