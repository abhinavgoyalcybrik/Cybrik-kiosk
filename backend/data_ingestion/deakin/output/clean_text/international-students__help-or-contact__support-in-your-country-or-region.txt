Deakin University international offices
With offices around the world, our friendly and knowledgeable staff can support you in your home country or region before you arrive in Australia. They can give you the latest information on our degrees, entry requirements and life at Deakin. They can also provide career advice and answer any questions you might have about studying abroad.

Deakin University authorised agents
Most of our students engage with a Deakin authorised international agent. They are trained to provide advice and information on our degrees and assist you with the application process.
If you are unsure whether the person you are in contact with is an authorised agent of Deakin University, please don't hesitate to contact us for verification.
