Do I need an authorised agent?
Deakin strongly recommends using an authorised agent. Agents make the application process easier for international students. They also help avoid common visa mistakes and can guide you through the online visa application process.
We suggest you only work with Deakin authorised agents. They have a signed agreement with the University and are regularly monitored to ensure high standards.
Students from certain countries must apply through an agent to meet Australian student visa requirements. These countries include:
- Africa (except Mauritius)
- Bangladesh
- Bhutan
- Cambodia
- India
- Iran
- Laos
- Nepal
- Pakistan
- Philippines
- Sri Lanka
- Thailand
- Vietnam
Learn more about student visa requirements for international students in Australia.
